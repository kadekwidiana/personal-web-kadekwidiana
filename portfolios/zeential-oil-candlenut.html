Zeential Oil - Candlenut Oil Product Website



The Zeential.oil website is a landing page site designed to provide complete information about candlenut oil products. This site presents various information such as uses, benefits, prices, and product descriptions, making it easier for users to understand the benefits and advantages of candlenut oil.

This site is equipped with a multilingual feature that supports three languages, namely Indonesian, Malay, and English, to reach more users. In addition, there is a contact feature that is directly integrated with WhatsApp, so customers can easily communicate with this service.
