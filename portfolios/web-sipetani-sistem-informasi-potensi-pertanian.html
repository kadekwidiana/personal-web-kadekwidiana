Web Sipetani (Sistem Informasi Potensi Pertanian)



Web Sipetani is a web-based system designed to facilitate access and management of information related to agricultural potential in Buleleng Regency. This system provides convenience for the general public, Agricultural Extension Workers (PPL), and administrators from the Buleleng Regency Agriculture Service in obtaining accurate and up-to-date agricultural data. With information presented visually and interactively, Sipetani supports better decision-making for the management and development of agricultural potential in the region.

This system is equipped with various main features, such as the presentation of agricultural geographic information, geographic data analysis, management of agricultural institutional data, management of land data, farmers, extension workers, and spatial data. In addition, Sipetani also provides features for recording and managing agricultural extension activities and managing reports. Various other additional features also support the process of visualizing and managing agricultural data to be more comprehensive and efficient.

Technically, Sipetani adopts the MVC (Model-View-Controller) architectural pattern which allows this system to work well and is easy to manage. The implementation of the latest technology in system development ensures reliable performance, high scalability, and ease of data management to meet the needs of users in the agricultural sector of Buleleng Regency.
